Homemade Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is a classic spaghetti recipe that can be made to satisfy the craving of a timeless classic. Spagheetti with tomato sauce and ground beef.

Ingredients

    1 Pound ground beef
    1 medium onion
    4 cloves garlic, minced
    1 small green bell pepper, diced
    1 (28ounce ) can diced tomatoes
    1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
    1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
    2 tspn dried oregano
    1 tspn dried basil
    1 tspn salt
    1/2 tspn ground black pepper
